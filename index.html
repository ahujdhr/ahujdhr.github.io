Introduction

Welcome to my Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) ePortfolio! I'm an enthusiastic undergraduate student exploring the dynamic intersection of electrical systems, computing, and problem‑solving. This website serves as a digital showcase of my academic journey, technical projects, and professional aspirations. Here you'll find insights into who I am, what I've accomplished, and where I'm headed.

Throughout my time at Georgia Tech, I've embraced opportunities to design hardware, write software, and collaborate on multidisciplinary teams. My goal is to connect with mentors, peers, and potential employers who share a passion for innovation. This ePortfolio is more than just a repository of assignments – it's a living document that highlights my growth, reflects on my experiences, and charts a path toward a fulfilling career.

Thank you for visiting. Use the navigation above to learn more about me, view my résumé, explore my career goals, and discover the projects I've worked on.
